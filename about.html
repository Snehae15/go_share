About

Go Share Application Service


< back

Mobile application provides a seamless and convenient way of using shared vehicles in wide verity of ways which does not limit to carpooling alone the system also provides services like Shared movement of goods, rental vehicles & Group trips. Through the app, users can get all the services without paying any amount in cash, Users will be able to Book carpooling & Bike pooling services while the app also allows users to book Goods movement irrespective of the size of the goods moved. Apart from all these users can also book car rentals and group trips through the app. Application works by integrating with the local businesses since the services are free of cost there will be a huge user base for the application, we take ads from different businesses and provide shopping vouchers and discount coupons for the vehicle owners for sharing their vehicle. The system has three major areas like Car Pooling, Goods transportation including small packages and heavy loads, Rentals & Group Trips. The system can be considered as a non-profit initiative to ease the transportation system and save the environment while reducing the number of vehicles on road which helps reduce the traffic


• Deduction, reasoning, problem solving - To implement a shared vehicle system that helps travelers and vehicle owners
• Knowledge representation - A good knowledge of the existing system and various options of sharing vehicles to be known.
• Reducing Traffic - The system helps in reduce the traffic on road by providing users an option to share the vehicles instead of travelling individually.
• Reduce Environment Pollution - As the number of vehicles gets reduced there will be a significant reduction in environment pollution.
